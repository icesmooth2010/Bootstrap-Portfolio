What I Rather Be Doing

What I wish I was doing right now instead of typing about myself is running (more like walking)on the Green River trail up in Kent, Washington right now.That place is my own peace of heaven. I know I may talk about the beach and how much I love the beach, but some of the best memories I every have is taking my two boys in a jogging stroller and pushing them over to the golf course on both rainy and sunny days. Heck even and night in the summer and going over to the driving range, picking up golf balls and throwing them back over the fence onto the range. We were poor, barley could move in our tiny townhouse because we moved way too much stuff into it, but I thin, fit, just barley got my health back and happy. My dream is to finish this program and find a great company or start up in Washington and return to my trail and return my children to a less tech active life. One in which we spend more time outside again and not so much time watching tv or cell phones and play on our trail we miss so much.
